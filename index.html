My Skills.

Circuit Design

Design and implementation of analog circuits like amplifiers as well as digital circuits such as A/D or D/A converters. After simulation and optimization in simulator softwares like Orcad or Proteus, practical Implementation is also carried out.

Switching Power Supply

Design and implementation of various configurations of switch mode power supplies such as buck, boost, flyback, etc. The first step is to simulate the circuits in LTspice or Hspice softwares. Then, practical implementation will be done.

Control and Monitoring

Design and implementation of digital/analog controllers for industrial proccesses such as boilers. Controllers include simple traditional PID controllers as well as advanced controllers such as MPC. Various softwares such as Matlab, Simulink, and Labview can be used for desinging controllers, and also monitoring the critical signals of the systems.

AI-based controllers

With growing complexity of industrial robots, new modern inteligent controllers are inevitably required. For example, reinforcement learning controllers can be used to control very complex control systems in an optimal manner and only through IO measured data.

System Identification

The first step in control of any industrial proccess is to identify its dynamics. According to the dynamics, optimal controllers can be designed. Various models like LTI models or nolinear ARX models can be used to model empirical data recorded from industrial proccesses.

Signal Proccessing

Digital and analog signal processing is among the most important prats of any embedded system. For example, FIR or IIR digital filters are required for noise cancellation. In another example, Kalman filters are essential to estimate the internal states of systems.
